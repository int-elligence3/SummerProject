CONNECT. SHARE. LEARN.

Motivation behind the project

During this pandemic, our lives are put on a pause! All of us had no choice but to study and work from home. But, the most difficult part of this journey is separation from our friends and social circle. Covid-19 has forced us to stay inside the homes, hence defeating the purpose of group learning via offline meets.

Our website helps in reducing this distancing by allowing people to CONNECT with their friends, SHARE notes and LEARN from each other by discussing their problems via virtual connections. Someone rightly said "Connection is all what humans need" and hence our website fulfills this purpose.

Features

Students are often confused from where to study. All they need is some concise study material to prepare for their exams. Our website helps in resolving all such problems. Here you'll get clear, concise and verified study notes to prepare for your exams. The notes are uploaded by either the professors themselves or the students with good handwriting.

Having photocopied notes for each topic of every subject sometimes becomes hectic to handle. It's troublesome to keep notes arranged in a proper order. Now, no more issues of handling big stacks of photocopied notes as you'll get everything here at one place. You'll find all the notes uploaded for each subject semester-wise.
