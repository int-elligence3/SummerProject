We at NoteBoat, are here to ensure your smooth sailing throughout your college years.

Your Problems

Some students have too much material to study from and they end up getting confused as to from where to study while some students don't have anything. All one needs is some concise study material to prepare for one's exams.

Having photocopied notes for each topic of every subject sometimes becomes hectic to handle. It's troublesome to keep notes arranged in a proper order.

Our Solution

Our website helps in resolving all such problems. Here you'll get clear, concise and verified study notes to prepare for your exams. The notes are uploaded by either the professors themselves or the students with good handwriting.

And no more issues of handling big stacks of photocopied notes as you'll get everything here at one place. You'll find all the notes uploaded for each subject semester-wise.
